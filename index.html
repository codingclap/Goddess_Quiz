Aura

The Greek goddess of the Breeze, Aura embodied the peaceful, relaxing feel of a fresh early morning. Borne of a Titan and water nymph, she was extraordinarily beautiful. Entirely comfortable in her own skin, Aura was an enchanting virgin huntress who spent her days artfully going where the winds would take her. Unfortunately, her free nature made her careless with her words, which ultimately led to her downfall.
